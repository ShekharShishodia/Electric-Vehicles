How Electric Vehicles Work.

Key Components of an All-Electric Car

1.Battery Pack: Stores electrical energy to power the vehicle.
2.Electric Motor: Converts electrical energy into mechanical energy to drive the wheels.
3.Power Inverter: Converts DC from the battery to AC for the motor.
4.Onboard Charger: Converts AC from the charging station to DC for the battery.
5.Charging Port: Allows the car to connect to a power source for recharging.
6.Transmission: Transfers power from the motor to the wheels.
7.Thermal Management System: Maintains optimal operating temperatures.
8.DC-DC Converter: Steps down high-voltage battery power to lower voltages for accessories.
9.Battery Management System (BMS): Monitors and regulates the battery's health and safety.
10.Regenerative Braking System: Captures energy during braking to recharge the battery.
